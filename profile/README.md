# X-Tour
 
Early-stage AI company working on domain-specific problems in finance and biotech.
 
---
 
### What We Believe
 
**Transparency over confidence** <br/>
We are honest about what our models can and cannot do. In high-stakes domains, knowing the limits of a system is as important as knowing its capabilities.
 
**Responsible AI is not a constraint - it is a design principle** <br/>
We think carefully about how much autonomy models should have. Not to limit what AI can do, but to ensure it operates in the right contexts with the right boundaries.
 
**Technology should make tomorrow better** <br/>
We are living through one of the most significant technological shifts in history. That comes with responsibility - to build carefully, honestly, and with real impact in mind.
 
---
 
### Open Source
 
We share tools, benchmarks, and utilities where we can. Core models and proprietary systems stay private.
